# Church Event Sample

## Title: Community Fellowship Potluck

## Event Type: Church Fellowship Meal

## Main Message

Join us for an evening of food, fellowship, and friendship! Our quarterly community potluck is a time to gather as a church family and welcome new faces. Bring your favorite dish to share — main course, side dish, or dessert. Everyone is invited, whether you've been attending for years or this is your first time through our doors. Kids are welcome, and we'll have activities for all ages. Come hungry, leave full — in body and spirit!

## Event Details

Date: Friday, June 12 at 6:30 PM
Location: Fellowship Hall, Community Church
Address: 123 Main Street
CTA: Bring a dish to share and a friend! Sign up at the welcome desk or just show up.

## Additional Info

- Childcare provided for ages 0-5
- Games and activities for kids 6-12
- Coffee and beverages provided by the church
