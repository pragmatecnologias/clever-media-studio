# Devotional Pack Sample

## Title: Strength for the Weary

## Passage: Isaiah 40:31

But those who hope in the Lord will renew their strength. They will soar on wings like eagles; they will run and not grow weary, they will walk and not be faint.

## Main Message

Are you tired? Not just physically — tired in your soul. The kind of weariness that sleep doesn't fix and coffee can't reach. God sees your weariness. He knows the weight you're carrying, the prayers you've prayed that seem unanswered, the hope that's wearing thin. Isaiah 40:31 doesn't promise an instant escape from hard circumstances. It promises something better: renewed strength for the journey. When you wait on God — when you trust His timing instead of demanding your own — He renews you from the inside out. You don't just survive. You soar. You run. You walk without fainting. This isn't positive thinking — it's a promise from the God who never grows tired or weary.

## Intended Use

This is a devotional message to be shared as social media posts throughout the week. No live event — purely digital encouragement.
