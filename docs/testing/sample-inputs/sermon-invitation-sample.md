# Sermon Invitation Sample

## Title: Held by His Hand

## Passage: Psalm 37:23-24

The Lord makes firm the steps of those who delight in Him; though they stumble, they will not fall, for the Lord upholds them with His hand.

## Main Message

God guides His people step by step through uncertain seasons. When life feels unstable — job changes, health scares, family struggles — we are not walking alone. The same God who holds the universe holds your hand. This message is about trusting God's guidance even when you can't see the next step. He doesn't promise a smooth path, but He promises His presence on every step of it. When you stumble, He catches you. When you're weak, He strengthens you. When you're lost, He leads you home.

1. God orders the steps of those who delight in Him

3. God's hand upholds us when we cannot stand
4. Your uncertain season is God's opportunity to show His faithfulness
