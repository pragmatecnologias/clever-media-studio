# Youth Program Sample

## Title: Unshakable — Building Faith That Lasts

## Passage: Matthew 7:24-25

Everyone who hears these words of mine and puts them into practice is like a wise man who built his house on the rock. The rain came down, the streams rose, and the winds blew and beat against that house; yet it did not fall, because it had its foundation on the rock.

## Main Message

Your faith is going to be tested. Not maybe — definitely. School pressure, friendship drama, social media comparison, questions about your future, doubts about your beliefs. These storms are real. But here's the truth: a faith built on God's Word doesn't collapse when life shakes. Jesus said the wise builder digs deep and builds on rock. The foolish builder takes shortcuts and builds on sand. Same storm hits both houses — but only one stands. What's your foundation? Is it your parents' faith? Your church attendance? Your good behavior? Those are sand. The rock is hearing Jesus' words AND putting them into practice.
